How It Works

Quality care in three simple steps

No waiting rooms. No phone tag. Just healthcare that works around your schedule, not the other way around.

1

Choose your provider

Browse top-rated, board-certified providers who match your needs. Read reviews, check availability, and pick the perfect fit.

2

Meet virtually or in-person

Connect face-to-face via video from your couch, or visit a Clarity clinic near you. Same great providers, your choice of format.

3

Get care, prescriptions, referrals

Receive a care plan tailored to you. Prescriptions are sent to your pharmacy or your door, and referrals are handled seamlessly.

Services

Comprehensive care, all in one place

From routine check-ups to specialized support, our providers are here for every part of your health journey.

Primary Care

Annual physicals, sick visits, preventive screenings, and ongoing wellness support with a provider who knows you.

Mental Health

Therapy and psychiatry from licensed professionals. Individual sessions, medication management, and ongoing support.

Dermatology

Skin concerns addressed quickly with photo-based visits. Acne, rashes, moles, and cosmetic consultations, all from home.

Chronic Care

Ongoing management for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and more. Regular check-ins and coordinated treatment plans.
